Normal paragraph example

Therapy is a great option to help children find coping mechanisms, learn emotion regulation, share feelings, combat mental illness, and much more. All of us need someone to talk to including our children. Their mental health is vital to their lives. Finding ways to do things such as regulate our emotions will follow use throughout our lives. So, lets start focusing on this now to create habit and strengthen neuropathways.

Colored paragraph example

Therapy is important for all ages. Children are finding their place and therapy can help with this. Their are different types of therapy such as art, music, animal, and much more. In my program, children will have fun and make progress!
